📊 Special Records & Trivia

Most Goals in a Single Tournament (Individual)
Just Fontaine (France)
13 goals in the 1958 World Cup. This record has stood unbroken for over 60 years.
Most Goals Across Most Tournaments
Miroslav Klose (Germany)
Scored in all 4 tournaments (2002–2014) he played. 16 career goals is the all-time record.
Youngest World Cup Scorer
Pelé (Brazil)
Pelé scored at 17 years and 239 days in 1958, long the youngest scorer record.
Top Active Scorer (as of 2022)
Kylian Mbappé (France)
12 goals in 2 tournaments (2018, 2022). Widely expected to challenge Klose's record at the 2026 WC.
Back-to-Back Tournament Top Scorer
Teófilo Cubillas (Peru)
5 goals each in 1970 and 1978, a remarkable feat 8 years apart.
Top Scorer + Champion in the Same Tournament
Paolo Rossi (1982), Mario Kempes (1978), Gerd Müller (1974), etc.
Rare cases when the tournament's top scorer also won the championship.
